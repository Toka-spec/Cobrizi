Presley Chweneyagae is a celebrated actor, director, and storyteller. Best known for his role in Tsotsi, he continues to inspire through performance and activism.

About Presley

Presley Chweneyagae is a South African actor known for his powerful performances in both film and theatre. His breakout role in Tsotsi won the 2005 Academy Award for Best Foreign Language Film. Presley is also a director and community leader focused on empowering youth.
