About Presley

Presley Chweneyagae is a South African actor known for his powerful performances in both film and theatre. He made global headlines after starring in Tsotsi, which won the 2005 Academy Award. He remains a passionate activist, storyteller, and director focused on inspiring the next generation.

Work & Achievements

  • Tsotsi – Academy Award-winning film
  • The River – Acclaimed South African drama series
  • Stage Director – Political and youth-focused theatre
  • Community Leader – Art initiatives and mentorship
